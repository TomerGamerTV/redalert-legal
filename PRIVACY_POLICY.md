# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-03-08  
**Service:** RedAlert-Bot  
**Owner/Operator (Data Controller):** TomerGamerTV

This Privacy Policy explains how RedAlert-Bot (the "Bot") processes information when you use the Bot.

## 1) Scope
This Policy applies to information processed through RedAlert-Bot on Discord servers and related operations.

## 2) Information we process
The Bot processes only the data needed for functionality, operations, and reliability:

### a) Discord identifiers and metadata
- User IDs
- Server (guild) IDs
- Channel IDs
- Role IDs
- Message IDs

### b) Configuration and preference data
- Server-level settings (enabled channels, roles, alert preferences)
- Command/feature configuration created by server administrators/moderators

### c) Operational and diagnostic data
- Command usage metadata (timestamps, success/failure, basic errors)
- Service logs needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the Bot

### d) Functional minimum data
- Other technical data strictly required for the Bot to function properly.

### e) What we do **not** process by default
- No optional analytics profile building.
- No sale of personal data.

## 3) Why we process data
We process data to:
- Provide and operate the Bot's core functionality.
- Deliver alerts/notifications based on server configuration.
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot errors.
- Keep the Bot stable and reliable.
- Comply with applicable legal obligations.

## 4) Legal framework
This Policy is intended for operation under applicable law in Israel. GDPR-specific compliance statements are not provided as a dedicated framework in this document.

## 5) Data sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only:
- With infrastructure providers required to run the Bot (Docker-based deployment on owner-controlled hosting).
- When required by law, legal process, or lawful government request.
- To protect rights, safety, security, and prevent fraud/abuse.

## 6) Data retention
- Bot-related IDs, configuration, and logs may be retained indefinitely ("to infinity and beyond") for continued operation, reliability, and historical troubleshooting.
- Data may be deleted at the operator's discretion or when legally required.

## 7) Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; therefore, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 8) International processing
Data may be processed in locations associated with owner-controlled hosting and supporting infrastructure.

## 9) User rights
Depending on applicable law, users may have rights related to their personal data. Where required, requests will be handled according to legal obligations and technical feasibility.

## 10) Children's privacy
The Bot is not directed to children below the minimum age required by Discord or local law.

## 11) Third-party services
Discord and external data providers have their own policies and terms. We are not responsible for third-party privacy or data handling practices.

## 12) Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

## 13) Contact
